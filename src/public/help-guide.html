5. Mock Interview

Practice your interview skills with AI-powered mock interviews and receive professional feedback.

Configure your mock interview with role, experience level, and duration

Starting an Interview

Navigate to Mock Interview

Click "Mock Interview" in the navigation bar to access the interview practice feature.

Configure Your Session

Select your target role (e.g., Software Engineer, Product Manager), experience level (Entry, Mid-level, Senior), and interview duration.

Start Interview

Click "Start Interview" to begin. The AI interviewer will ask you questions relevant to your role and experience level.

Answer Questions

Type your responses in the text area provided. Take your time ‚Äì there's no strict time limit per question.

Receiving Feedback

After completing all questions, you'll receive comprehensive feedback including:

  • Overall Score: Your performance rating out of 100
  • Strengths: What you did well in your responses
  • Improvements: Areas where you can enhance your answers
  • Professional Assessment: Detailed rubric-based evaluation
